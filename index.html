A Little About Me!

Growing up in the incredibly scenic Columbia River Gorge in Hood River, Oregon, I have learned to never take anything for granted.

I consider myself extremely lucky to have had such a naturally beautiful place right in my backyard where I could learn how to mountain bike, hike, and always have great things to do outside.

One day I would love to make my dreams a reality, and combine my passion for being outdoors with my second passion, technology(I know, it doesn't make a whole lot of sense). This is why I chose to attend The Tech Academy, a coding bootcamp based out of Portland, Oregon.

Computers(large or small) have always fascinated me. I love learning about how they work, about all of the infinite possibilities they have just waiting to be unlocked, and the sheer amount of impact that they have on nearly every single person on the planet.

That is why I want to primarily focus on the experience of the user! Although I like to be professional, I REALLY enjoy keeping things lighthearted; both with what I develop as well as the people I make friends with(scroll up and look at my nose again, I know you want to). I think a great workplace is somewhere trust builds to the point where everyone is encouraged to be themselves in a creative environment.
